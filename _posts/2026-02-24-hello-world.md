Welcome to the blog. I've been meaning to start writing for a while now, and today's the day.

This space will be home to my thoughts on the work I do every day: building custom software, implementing AI systems, and helping businesses make smarter technology decisions. I plan to cover topics like:

- **Software architecture** — patterns and trade-offs I encounter in real projects
- **AI implementation** — practical lessons from integrating LLMs and machine learning into production systems
- **Engineering craft** — the small decisions that compound into great (or terrible) software
- **Business of technology** — bridging the gap between technical capability and business outcomes

I believe the best writing comes from doing the work first and reflecting on it second. So expect posts grounded in experience rather than theory.

If you're building something and want to talk through it, don't hesitate to [reach out](/#contact). More to come soon.
